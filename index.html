Rules

The rules are simple! All you need is to spot the highest number out of four random numbers between 1 and 100. You can use either arrow keys on your keyboard for controls or click on the numbers. If two or more numbers are equal, you can choose either of them. If you get it right, the numbers will change, the timer restart, and you get one point. If you get it wrong, the game will be over. The amount of time for making a choice decreases over time. To start the game, click on the "Start the game" button on the main screen. Good luck!
